### ARIES Science Club

*07 April, 2026*

---
**World Health Day** is celebrated every year on **April 7** to mark the establishment of the **World Health Organization (WHO)** in 1948. This day aims to spread awareness about important global health issues and encourage people to pay attention to their **physical and mental well-being**. A healthy life depends not only on proper medication but also on **daily habits and routines**.

### Annual Theme

Each year, a specific theme is chosen to highlight a major health concern affecting people worldwide, such as disease prevention, mental health, or access to healthcare. This year’s theme, **“Together for health. Stand with science,”** emphasizes the importance of collective efforts and scientific guidance in improving health outcomes.

### Celebration at ARIES

Our institute, **ARIES**, also celebrated **World Health Day** to highlight the importance of collective well-being and the role of science in promoting a healthier society. We sought guidance from **researchers and scientists** on how they maintain their mental and physical health despite their heavy research work and hectic schedules.

The goal of our **Science Club** is to spread this knowledge and awareness throughout society. The snapshots below capture moments from the **video** prepared for World Health Day, highlighting scientists’ and researchers’ perspectives on maintaining well-being.
