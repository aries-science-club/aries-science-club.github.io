ARIES SCIENCE CLUB

Our Mission

The ARIES Science Club is a joint initiative of ARIES and AcSIR. Science club is envisaged to be a platform where the AcSIR students can meet, share their ideas about science, culture of science, history of science, future of science, relation of science to the society, etc.
